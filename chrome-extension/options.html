About

Backup your Pokemon Showdown teams to the cloud. Restore your teams from any Chrome desktop browser.

Instructions

Usage

All interaction with this extension happens from a showdown import/export page.

Sign in

  • There is no "forgot password" functionality!
  • Extension accounts are *not* linked to your showdown accounts!
  • Your teams are password protected, but *not* encrypted!

  1. Open the extension .
  2. Enter a username and password. If your username/password combination isn't in the database, a new account will automatically be created.
  3. Accept the terms/directions checkbox
  4. Click the "Login" button

Save a team

  1. Build a team on showdown.
  2. Go to the import/export screen for that team.
  3. Open the extension .
  4. Goto the Save tab.
  5. Click the "BACKUP" button

Load a team

  1. Build and save a team on showdown.
  2. Go to the import/export screen for a new team.
  3. Open the extension .
  4. Goto the Load tab.
  5. Click the button
