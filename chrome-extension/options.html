About

Backup your Pokemon Showdown teams to the cloud. Restore your teams from any Chrome desktop browser.

Instructions

  1. From the Showdown teambuilder page...
  2. Either click button, or the edit team button.
  3. Click the button.
  4. Open the extension popup from the Chrome extension bar
  5. Click the button to backup your team to the cloud, or the link to load your team into Showdown.
