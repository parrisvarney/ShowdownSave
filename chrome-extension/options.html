About

Backup your Pokemon Showdown teams to the cloud. Restore your teams from any Chrome desktop browser.

Instructions

  1. Go to the Import/Export screen for a new team or an existing team
  2. Open the extension popup from the Chrome extension bar
  3. Click the "Backup" button to backup your team to the cloud, or "Load to import screen" link to load your team into the site.
